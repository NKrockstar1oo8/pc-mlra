# Extracted Clauses from Medical-Legal Documents

## NHRC Patient Charter - Key Clauses

### Section 3: Right to Information and Access
**3.4 Right to Access Medical Records**
- **Exact Text**: "Every patient has the right to access their medical records, including investigation reports, treatment records, and discharge summaries. Copies shall be provided within 72 hours of request."
- **Paraphrase**: Patients can get copies of their medical documents within 3 days of asking.
- **Rights**: access_medical_records, obtain_documents
- **Obligations**: doctor_provide_records, hospital_maintain_records
- **Actors**: patient, doctor, hospital
- **Exceptions**: Psychiatric records in certain cases
- **Keywords**: report, record, document, file, access, copy, medical history

### Section 4: Right to Consent
**4.1 Informed Consent**
- **Exact Text**: "No medical procedure shall be performed without obtaining free, informed, and specific consent from the patient. The doctor must explain the nature, risks, benefits, and alternatives of the procedure."
- **Paraphrase**: Doctors must explain treatments and get permission before doing any procedure.
- **Rights**: informed_consent, treatment_explanation
- **Obligations**: doctor_explain_procedure, obtain_consent
- **Actors**: patient, doctor
- **Exceptions**: Emergency situations where patient is unconscious
- **Keywords**: consent, permission, agree, explain, procedure, risk

## IMC Ethics Code - Key Clauses

### Section 7: Doctor-Patient Relationship
**7.2 Professional Behavior**
- **Exact Text**: "Physicians shall maintain professional decorum, avoid verbal abuse, and treat patients with dignity and respect regardless of background."
- **Paraphrase**: Doctors must be respectful and professional with all patients.
- **Rights**: respectful_treatment, dignity
- **Obligations**: maintain_professionalism, avoid_abuse
- **Actors**: patient, doctor
- **Exceptions**: None
- **Keywords**: respect, professional, behavior, abuse, shout, rude

**7.3 Confidentiality**
- **Exact Text**: "Patient information shall be kept confidential except when required by law or with patient consent. Medical records shall be secured against unauthorized access."
- **Paraphrase**: Doctors must keep patient information private and secure.
- **Rights**: privacy, confidentiality
- **Obligations**: maintain_confidentiality, secure_records
- **Actors**: patient, doctor, hospital
- **Exceptions**: Legal requirements, public health emergencies
- **Keywords**: privacy, confidential, secret, disclose, information, leak
